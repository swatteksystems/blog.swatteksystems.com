<p>Updating OpenSolaris b121 to b122 results in kernel panic (NOTICE: spa_import_rootpool: error 5)</p>
<div class="plain">
<p>Upgrading to the b122 bits on my laptop resulted in a kernel panic and reboots upon boot attempt. See this bug:<a href="http://bugs.opensolaris.org/bugdatabase/view_bug.do?bug_id=6875025" class="uri" class="external-link">http://bugs.opensolaris.org/bugdatabase/view_bug.do?bug_id=6875025</a></p>
<p>My work-around for this is to boot into my working BE (b121) and do the following:</p>
<p>beadm mount b122 /mnt</p>
<p>copy from /kernel to /mnt/kernel these bits:</p>
<p>/kernel/kmdb/amd64/sata<br />
/kernel/kmdb/sata<br />
/kernel/misc/sata<br />
/kernel/misc/amd64/sata</p>
<p>and these:</p>
<p>/kernel/drv/amd64/ahci<br />
/kernel/drv/ahci</p>
<p>then:</p>
<p>bootadm update-archive -R /mnt</p>
<p>My system then booted successfully to b122.</p>
